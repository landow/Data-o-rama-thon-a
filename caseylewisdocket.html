Ct requests ceremonial courtroom for multi-day trial. CC of this order to S Tatters. Parties shall forthwith schedule PTC with S Tatters. Scheduling Order No. 1 (Complex Case): The parties shall schedule a pretrial conference as provided above. The attorneys trying the case shall appear for said conference. Prior to the conference, the parties shall file all pretrial motions. Prior to trial, the parties shall file ; a dirty copy of the proposed instructions; a daily trial schedule setting forth estimated times for voir dire; opening; direct and cross examination of each witness; and closing. Prior to the commencement of the trial, each party shall provide to the Court the following: a complete exhibit list including sub-exhibits with the following columns: Description, ID, Offer, Admit, Deny, Exhibit and Comment (Plaintiff/State shall use numbers and Defendant shall use letters); a loose leaf notebook containing in numerical order copies of all exhibits (for civil cases only); a witness list; a lexicon of technical words and phrases to be used in the trial; an updated daily trial schedule; and an updated dirty copy of the instructions. Notwithstanding the foregoing, nothing herein shall require a party to disclose trial strategy and any of the above required documents may be filed with the court for the court's eyes only - the same being generally filed post trial for public viewing. go (DB)
